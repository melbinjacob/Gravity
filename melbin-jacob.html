Melbin Jacob: Capturing the Essence of Interior Design

Melbin Jacob is a talented photographer at Gravity Interiors, specializing in capturing stunning visuals that bring out the beauty of interior spaces.

His keen eye for detail and understanding of lighting techniques allow him to create compelling images that tell a story and evoke emotions.

Explore our portfolio to see more of Melbin Jacob's exceptional interior design photography.

About Melbin Jacob

Melbin has been working as a professional photographer for over 10 years, with a focus on interior design and architectural photography. He has a passion for creating images that showcase the unique elements and atmosphere of each space.

His work has been featured in several renowned magazines and publications, gaining recognition for his ability to capture the essence of a design and translate it into captivating visuals.

Photography Style

Melbin's photography style is characterized by his attention to detail and composition. He carefully composes each shot, considering the lines, textures, and colors within the frame.

His use of natural light combined with artificial lighting techniques allows him to create a balance that accentuates the key features of a space. The resulting images are vibrant and immersive, giving viewers a sense of being in the room.

Collaboration with Gravity Interiors

As part of the Gravity Interiors team, Melbin works closely with the design and marketing departments to bring their vision to life through his photography.

His images play a crucial role in showcasing the company's portfolio and attracting potential clients. Melbin's ability to capture the essence of each project helps convey the craftsmanship and attention to detail that Gravity Interiors is known for.
